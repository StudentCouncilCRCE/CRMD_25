Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering

Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (CRCE) is one of the premier institutes in Mumbai, imparting high-quality technical education. With a focus on academic excellence, human values, and holistic development, CRCE consistently produces competent and innovative engineers ready to meet industry demands.

The institution fosters a dynamic and inclusive environment, encourages ethical research, and maintains collaborations with academic institutions, industries, and professional societies. With its state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty, CRCE remains committed to nurturing responsible global citizens and future leaders.
