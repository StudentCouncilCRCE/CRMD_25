CRMD 2025 - The Grand Chessboard Tournament

About CRMD Chess Tournament

CRMD (Conceicao Rodrigues Memorial Department) 2025 is the premier chess tournament organized by FR. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering Student Council. This strategic gaming event brings together chess enthusiasts from across Mumbai for competitive matches, networking, and celebrating the art of chess.

Tournament Features

  • Competitive chess matches with professional supervision
  • Multiple tournament categories for different skill levels
  • Networking opportunities with fellow chess players
  • Awards and recognition for top performers
  • Educational workshops on chess strategy

Event Details

Join us at FR. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering for an unforgettable chess tournament experience. The event features strategic gameplay, competitive spirit, and community building among chess enthusiasts.
